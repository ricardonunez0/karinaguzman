Bio
I grew up in Southern California, always having a love for fashion. I began working in retail after high school, and by age 19, I was a visual merchandiser at Forever 21.

Putting together outfits for people, floors, and mannequins became my passion. I absolutely love helping clients find the right piece, the right silhouette, and the right fit,  building a real connection and seeing what fits for them, not what looks good to me.

This passion of mine, of finding the right outfit for the right person, made me realize how much I love styling as a career, and since then, I’ve been absorbing what seems like millions of different stylists’ takes on new seasons, new trends, and new themes.

I’m new to the career field of styling, but I’m not new to visual work, putting together recommendations, or bringing my love for fashion to every client I have, and I’m super excited to be transitioning to styling full time.
Thanks for stopping by,
I’d love to get in touch!
